Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Effective immediately upon app use

Xpenso does not collect, store, or share your personal financial data. Everything you enter stays on your device.

👋 Introduction

Xpenso is an offline-first expense tracking app developed by John. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your data. We believe privacy is a right, not a feature — so we've built Xpenso to keep your financial information entirely on your device.

📦 Data Collection

Xpenso does not collect or transmit your personal financial information. All expense entries, budgets, and categories you create are stored locally on your device only. We never upload, sync, or access this data.

📊 Analytics (Firebase)

To improve the app experience, Xpenso uses Firebase Analytics by Google to collect anonymous usage data. This includes:

  • App interactions such as screen views and button taps
  • Device type and operating system version
  • General usage patterns and behavior

This data does not include any of your financial information and does not personally identify users.

⚙️ Your Control

You can disable analytics at any time from within the app's Settings. Once disabled, no usage data will be collected from your device.

🚫 Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. The anonymous analytics data collected via Firebase is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

🔔 Permissions

Xpenso may request permission to send notifications to your device. This is used solely for optional expense reminders you set yourself. We do not send unsolicited messages or marketing notifications.

🛡️ Security

All your expense data is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device's built-in security (screen lock, encryption). We recommend keeping your device software up to date for the best protection.

✏️ Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will reflect the new date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
